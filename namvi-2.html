About Us

Nam Viet Gloves are a quality and reliable brand of disposable gloves and workwear products. The core of our success is building and sustaining relationships with our partners through our innovation, dedicated customer care, and a guarantee to deliver our promises. We are innovative, caring & attentive. Nam Viet Gloves Joint Stock Company is a company producing Medical Rubber Gloves in Vietnam. Starting to enter the field of manufacturing Medical Latex Gloves from 2015. Up to now, Nam Viet has developed with 24 production lines. In particular, 95% of output is exported to countries such as the US, Japan, Italy, Malaysia, Thailand, Singapore, ... Quality management system according to international standard ISO 9001 and Safety management system for Health products ISO 13485 has been recognized by Bureau Veritas (France) since 2016 to help control the entire production process. manufacturing, ensuring the supply of high quality products that exceed customer expectations and are completely environmentally friendly. The company has been gradually standardizing the entire process from production to business. Identify long-term strategic vision as well as build appropriate short-term steps to achieve strategic goals. Nam Viet always focuses on building human resources as its core values, always aiming to build a dynamic, friendly and professional working environment, creating many opportunities for success in career and career.
